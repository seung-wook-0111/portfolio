About Me

My Background

I work at the intersection of Computer Science, Design, and User Research. Drawing from these diverse disciplines, I create solutions that are logical in structure, aesthetically intentional, and deeply human-centered.

With a foundation in both technical problem-solving and thoughtful design thinking, I aim to build experiences that are clear, meaningful, and accessible.


My Hobby

I enjoy exploring creative disciplines. Beyond UX design, I’ve experimented with Korean traditional folk painting, woodworking, ceramics, calligraphy, and video editing. I’m also passionate about architecture—visiting inspiring buildings and crafting 3D architectural models is something I truly love.

I also enjoy traveling and outdoor adventures. Cycling, hiking, and caving are my favorite ways to explore new places and reset my mind.
